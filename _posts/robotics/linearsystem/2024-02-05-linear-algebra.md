## 6. Function of a Square Matrix

A : square matrix, k : positive integer,

$$
\mathbf{A}^k = \mathbf{A} \mathbf{A} \cdot\cdot\cdot \mathbf{A} (\text{k terms})
$$

$$
\mathbf{A}^0 = \mathbf{I}
$$

$f(\lambda) = \lambda^3 + 2\lambda^2 - 6$,

$$
f(\mathbf{A}) = \mathbf{A}^3 + 2\mathbf{A}^2 - 6
$$

$\mathbf{A}$ : block diagonal,

$$
\mathbf{A}^k = 
\begin{bmatrix} 
  \mathbf{A}_1 & 0  \\
  0 & \mathbf{A}_2  \\
\end{bmatrix}
,\;\;\;
f(\mathbf{A}) = 
\begin{bmatrix} 
  f(\mathbf{A}_1) & 0  \\
  0 & f(\mathbf{A}_2)  \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
