# Lipschiz Continuity

Definition

Function $g: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ is Lipschiz continuous if

$$
\vert g(x) - g(y) \vert \leq L \vert\vert x - y \vert\vert
$$

for some $L > 0$ and for all $x, y \in \mathbb{D} = \mathbb{R}^n$
